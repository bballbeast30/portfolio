Shop Your Store

  • An Android and iOS ecommerce app that gives every user their own “store”, so they can sell their items and “shop their store”.

  • This project is geared at people who want to sell off old clothes and those you don’t have the means to run their own website due to cost.

  • This app was built with the Flutter SDK as the front end and Cloud Firestore as the backend.
